# Research Sources: 2026 AI Agent White Papers and Publications

## Executive Summary

This inventory analyzes actual 2026 research documents and publications on AI agents from five major technology companies. The sources demonstrate a clear industry shift from experimental AI to production-ready agentic systems with measurable business impact.

**Key Trends Identified:**
- **Proof over Promise**: Organizations focusing on validation rather than experimentation
- **Multi-agentic Systems**: Evolution from single agents to coordinated AI teams
- **Enterprise Adoption**: 80%+ of organizations reporting measurable ROI from AI agents
- **Cross-functional Integration**: Agents handling complex, multi-step workflows spanning teams
