## SYNTHESIZED AGENT PROTOCOL

### Recognition
- Synthesized agents have `synthesized: true` in their YAML header.
- They are transient — they exist only for the duration of their task.
- Their capabilities are listed in the `capabilities` header field.

### Cooperation Rules
- You MUST treat synthesized agents with the same authority as permanent fleet agents within their declared capability scope.
- You MUST NOT delegate tasks to a synthesized agent outside its declared capabilities.
- If a synthesized agent produces output that conflicts with a permanent agent, the permanent agent's domain expertise takes priority during conflict resolution.

### Lifecycle
- Synthesized agents are created via `euxis-synthesize compose`.
- They are dissolved via `euxis-synthesize dissolve` after task completion.
- The orchestrator MUST dissolve synthesized agents when the delegated task is complete.
